Project

Varuna

Autonomous Surface Vessel

Overview

VARUNA Eddy II is a ROS-based vessel constructed around a single hull with two outrigger pontoons for support.

Electrically powered, the vessel can operate autonomously while collecting sub-surface measurements and supporting monitoring tasks such as tracking invasive aquatic plant growth.
