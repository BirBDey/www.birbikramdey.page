Project

DragonBall

Spherical Visually Guided Robot

Overview

DragonBall explores spherical robot design for hazardous environments where contamination resistance and simple decontamination are critical.

The research addresses locomotive control, sensor placement, and robust construction in a rolling-body architecture.

This work contributed to published results in RoManSy 2020: Robot Design, Dynamics and Control (pp. 305-312).
